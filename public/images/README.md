# Frontend images

Place image files here to use them on the frontend.

**Usage in the app:** Reference by path from the site root, e.g.:

- `/images/your-file.png`
- In JSX: `<img src="/images/your-file.png" alt="..." />`

**Suggested filenames for your assets:**

- `about-us.png` – About us / achievements photo (three people, Supercomputing Challenge)
- `finalist-teams.png` – Group photo with finalist banners (LANL / Sandia)
- `lanl-logo.png` – Los Alamos National Laboratory logo
- `supercomputing-challenge-logo.png` – Supercomputing Challenge triangle logo
- `triad-logo.png` – TRIAD National Security, LLC logo
- `nmped-logo.png` – NMPED logo

Copy or move your image files into this folder, then use the paths above in your components.
